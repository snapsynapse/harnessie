# AIDR-0000: Short noun phrase naming the decision

## Context

Why this decision exists and what is at stake. Keep it under 200 words. Put links in Evidence.

## Question

One decidable question, stated so a reader can tell whether a position answers it.

## Positions

### Position: agent-label

- agent: agent-label
- model: model-id-as-reported
- provider: vendor-name
- stance: recommend
- summary: One sentence stating the position.

Prose argument for the position. Written independently; if this position was formed after reading others, say so here.

### Position: second-agent-label

- agent: second-agent-label
- model: model-id-as-reported
- provider: different-vendor
- stance: oppose
- summary: One sentence stating the position.

Prose argument. Two positions from distinct providers qualify the record for the independent-positions claim.

## Objections

### Objection: agent-label to Position second-agent-label

Concrete failure mode, contradiction, or risk. Generic preference belongs in Positions, not here. Objections are never removed once recorded.

## Arbitration

- decided_by: Your Name
- date: 2026-01-02
- decision: One sentence stating what was decided.

Human-authored rationale. Must address every objection above: accepted, rejected with reason, or deferred with a named follow-up. While status is open, leave this section empty (heading only) and omit the decided frontmatter field.

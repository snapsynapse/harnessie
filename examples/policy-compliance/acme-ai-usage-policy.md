# ACME Corp, AI Usage Policy (sample document, v1.2)

## 1. Scope

This policy applies to all ACME employees and contractors using AI tools for work purposes.

## 2. Approved tools

Employees may use the AI tools listed in the IT Service Catalog. Use of unlisted tools requires written approval from the IT Security team. The catalog entry for each tool names a business owner and its approved purposes.

## 3. Data handling

Confidential client data must not be entered into AI tools unless the tool is listed as "client-data approved" in the catalog. Employees are responsible for verifying a tool's data classification before use.

## 4. Vendor review

New AI vendors undergo the standard third-party security review. The review covers data residency, retention, and model-training clauses in the vendor's terms.

## 5. Training

All employees complete the annual "Responsible AI at ACME" e-learning module. Line managers are responsible for completion rates in their teams.

## 6. Violations

Suspected violations of this policy are reported to the IT Security team via the standard incident process (SEC-INC-01).

# Obligations to assess (sample)

Derived from a generic AI-governance baseline. One finding per obligation is required.

1. The organization maintains an inventory of AI systems in use, including purpose and owner.
2. High-risk AI use cases require documented human oversight before deployment.
3. Personal data used for model training or prompting has a documented lawful basis.
4. Vendors providing AI capabilities are assessed before procurement.
5. Employees receive guidance on approved and prohibited AI tool usage.
6. AI-related incidents have a defined reporting and response path.
